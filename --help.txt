Usage: cat [OPTION]... [FILE]... Concatenate FILE(s) to standard output.

With no FILE, or when FILE is -, read standard input.

 Examples:

documentation <https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/cat> or available
locally via: info '(coreutils) cat invocation'
